Overview & Context
As physician shortages rise, employers are recruiting more short-term providers to fill their staffing needs. Kimedics is a web and mobile network for provider recruitment and scheduling.
Who uses Kimedics?
Three types of professionals use Kimedics.
1. Healthcare organizations: they need qualified providers to fill their shifts
2. Staffing organizations: they match providers with work opportunities
3. Healthcare providers: including physicians, NPs, PAs, CRNAs
Design research
The Kimedics founder has a deep knowledge from years of working in physician management. As a starting point, we discuss typical workflows and painpoints to understand the problem space and pull in key quotes from target users to guide our priorities.

Sample user flow
Task - A manager is filling open (i.e. unstaffed) shifts. From interviews, we know that they communicate via email and phone about availability currently so we wanted to build this into the mobile app to save managers time.
